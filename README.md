# Optimisation-Model
Three business LP problems solved with SciPy's HiGHS solver: production planning (maximise profit under machine/material/labour constraints), staff scheduling (minimise headcount while covering daily demand), and transportation (minimise shipping cost across 3 warehouses → 4 stores). Total shipping cost optimised to $4,150.
